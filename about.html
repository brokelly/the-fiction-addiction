About The Fiction Addiction

CLICK HERE

Fiction Addiction is a vibrant online hub dedicated to all things fiction. Whether you're an avid reader or simply searching for your next favorite novel, the site provides book reviews, discussion blogs, author spotlights, and carefully curated reading lists. Our goal is to create a welcoming community where readers can discover new stories, share opinions, and celebrate the books they love.

Meet The Team

Brooke Kelly

I am a lover of fiction just like you! I've fallen in love with characters who don't exist, cried over plot twists, and occasionally wished real life came with a better author. If there's a fantasy world with dragons or morally gray heroes, chances are I've already packed my bags.

Sassy McFrizzle

Hugh Jass

Phil McCracken
