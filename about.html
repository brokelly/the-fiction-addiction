About The Fiction Addiction


CLICK HERE

Fiction Addiction is a vibrant online hub dedicated to all things fiction. Whether you're an avid reader or simply looking for your next great book, the website offers a wealth of content to fuel your literary passion. It features engaging book club blogs where readers can connect, discuss, and dive deeper into their favorite novels. The site also showcases carefully curated top fiction lists, helping you discover the best books across various genres. From author interviews to book reviews, Fiction Addiction brings together everything you need to explore and celebrate the world of fiction.


Meet The Team

Sassy McFrizzle
I'm basically a mix between a confused philosopher and a sleep-deprived raccoon. I thrive on snacks, sarcasm, and highly questionable life choices. My daily exercise includes dodging responsibilities, jumping to conclusions, and running out of patience. I have the memory of a goldfish, the energy of a sloth, and the hunger of a small bear preparing for hibernation. Approach with caution—side effects may include excessive laughter and secondhand embarrassment. Let me know if you want more!
Hugh Jass
