📖 Book of the Month

A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J. Maas follows Feyre Archeron, a young huntress whose life changes forever after she unknowingly kills a faerie wolf. She is taken to the magical land of Prythian, where she discovers powerful High Fae, dangerous creatures, ancient curses, and a world far different than the stories she grew up hearing.

Filled with romance, adventure, mystery, and unforgettable characters, this bestselling fantasy series has become one of the most popular modern fiction series and is a favorite among fantasy readers around the world.
